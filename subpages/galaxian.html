Galaxian in Webgl

What

A capstone project for my Computer Graphics course. We were tasked with creating a simple version of the classic Galaxian video game, applying the skills we learned in the course in WebGL programming. Unfortunately, I believe it would violate some university policies to include the actual project, but I may check with my instructors since it wouldn't be difficult and would make for a more effective demo than a screenshot.

Why

The main motivation for this project was as a capstone assessment for the skills learned in the graphics course. However, game development is the spark that got me interested in computer science initially, and it would be a lie to say I didn't take to this project with some extra passion. I enjoyed the 'game engine design' aspects of this project immensely, more so than the graphics and rendering.

Results

I got a solid grade on this project, and I really enjoyed working on it. The few twists and quality of life features/effects I added, including the score/round counters, explosions, and enemy animation effects, as well as the difficulty ramp-up, were a lot of fun to come up with and hardcode in. I think the final result is at the least playable, and maybe even a bit fun. It demonstrates a solid understanding of computer graphics and the basics of how to structure a game in code.
